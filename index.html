The Messy Disc Golf Project

Your home for all things disc golf

Your one stop shop for discs, gear, tips, and other disc golf resources. Help us grow the sport by introducing new players and finding more time for your own rounds! No matter how long you have been playing, or your skill level, here you can find help with things like:

  • Building your bag
  • Learning how to gain more distance
  • Improving your putting form
  • Starting your own tournament or league
  • Networking

And so much more! we are happy you're here. Thank you for making us part of your disc golf journey.
